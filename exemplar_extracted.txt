foster innovation in Jordan's fintech sector. Only 33% of adults have formal financial

government and corporate sector are supporting fintech through significant funding [27
– 29]. The collaboration between financial institutions and fintech companies shows the
commitment to a strong digital financial marketplace.

delivering an innovative and simple alternative to traditional banking and ATM services.

[30].
